# C38 · Crunch Scale — Brand Guide

- **Accent:** Code Crunch blue `#2463EB` (with deep `#1d4ed8`).
- **Voice:** operator-rigorous and hype-free. Every growth claim comes with the query that proves it. No vanity metrics, no dashboard theater — show the funnel, the cohort, the experiment, and the number that would change the decision. Growth is a measured system, not a growth-hack.
- **Icon motif:** a funnel narrowing into a rising cohort curve.
- **One-liner:** "Grow revenue on evidence — funnels, cohorts, and experiments, measured in SQL."
